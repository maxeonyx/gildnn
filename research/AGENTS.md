# research/

Distilled verified discovery — not reports, not logs. Everything in here that Max reads should earn its place.

## Structure

- `daily/YYYY-MM-DD.md` — daily narrative produced at 4pm. What ran, what it produced, what changed.
- `weekly/YYYY-MM-DD.md` — weekly synthesis produced at 4pm on Thursdays. Significant findings, evidence, honest assessment.
- `questions/` — one folder per open question or investigation thread.

## Quality standard

Everything in here that Max reads must meet distill.pub standard. See [PROCESS.md](../PROCESS.md#reporting) for the full requirement.

Short version: every claim backed by a real artifact, embedded inline in the markdown. Max should be able to read the file without clicking anything — the link proves it's real, but the content is right there. No free-floating assertions. Iterate heavily before calling a narrative done.
